About Us

Water Cannon Fountains began with inspiration from my grandfather—a proud veteran and master craftsman who spent over 20 years hand-making pond fountains by hand. I had the privilege of working beside him for five of those years, learning the artistry, precision, and pride that went into each creation.

Watching his dedication and the joy his fountains brought to families and communities planted the seed for what would eventually grow into this business. I created Water Cannon Fountains to honor his legacy and share the beauty of handcrafted water features with even more people.

Today, we continue that tradition—blending timeless craftsmanship with modern design. Whether it's transforming a garden or elevating a commercial space, our fountains are made to inspire peace, elegance, and a touch of magic.

Thank you for being a part of this journey. We're excited to help you bring your vision to life—one fountain at a time.
